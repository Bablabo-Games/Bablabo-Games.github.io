What's all the goss about?

Hush Hush High is a weird stealth game where you play as Mochi, a gossip monger who can't keep her mouth shut. Step into the mind of Mochi as she navigates through the corridors of her high school and help her focus on her studies

It's your responsibility to control her urge for spilling every piece of tantalizing gossip to her fellow schoolmates.

Meet Mochi!

Daily Classes

Attend your classes and run some errands around the highschool.

Remember to focus on your studies!

Gossip spread

Don't tell the gossip to anyone! Mochi can't keep any secrets so it's your job to keep her away from students.

Remember: Gossip spreads like wildfire.

Mini Games

Play a variety of mini-games that test your reflexes to stop Mochi from opening her mouth.

KNOW THEIR SECRETS, BUT DON'T TELL ANYONE!
