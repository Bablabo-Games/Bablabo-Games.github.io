About the Game

Hush Hush High is a quirky stealth game where you play as Mochi, a gossip monger who can't keep her mouth shut. Step into the mind of Mochi as she navigates through the corridors of her high school and help her focus on her studies.

It's your responsibility to control her urge for spilling every piece of tantalizing gossip to her fellow schoolmates.

Features

  • Complete tasks and attend classes in the Highschool.
  • Keep Mochi away from other students!
  • Control Mochi's urge to spill gossip by playing a bunch of minigames.
  • Find the optimal way to complete all your tasks and get the highest score!

MEET THE TEAM

We are a team of 3 game designers from Bengaluru, India working on Hush Hush High. The three of us met at our previous job, where we instantly bonded over our love for deep and creative games.

Bored out of our minds, we participated in the Pirate Software Game Jam 2024. We overshot the deadline of the game jam, but all of us really loved the concept and hence we decided to make it into a full game.

Hush Hush High has been a learning experience for us as this is our first game to be published on Steam. It has also been a creative outlet for us, to have fun with whacky ideas.

We hope our players enjoy the game as much as we did while working on this game!
